Welcome to Auto 4 Sale

Auto 4 Sale buys various of classic cars from all around the world in attempt to make them "brand new" again. They are being tuned with the best features available in the motor industry to re-invent them and be back in demand once again.

The Auto 4 Sale company was founded in 1990 when our first Bmw Dealership was established in Durban. The company has since acquired Volkswagen, Audi, Nissan and many other franchises and currently operates dealerships all around the world.

The cornerstone of this well-balanced motor group is the important of family values in an everchanging business environment. We are passionate about our valued customers and professional staff who deliver a service matched by no other. We aim to make a difference in the lives of our customers, our staff members and the area we service.
